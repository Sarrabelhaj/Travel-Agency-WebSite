Odyssey Travel

Odyssey Travel, LLC was born out of what founder, Jennifer Jacob, saw as a necessity. Travel has shifted from being about "seeing" to being more about "feeling." We infuse each trip with culture and local flair, while considering the style and budget of each traveler. Your vacation is destined to be as unique as you are.
